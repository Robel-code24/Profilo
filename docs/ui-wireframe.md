# UI Wireframe

## Layout
- Fixed top navigation with brand mark and anchor links
- Hero section occupying the first viewport with animated intro and CTA buttons
- Content sections separated by subtle dividers and glass panels
- Footer with social links and a back-to-top action

## Landing Page Structure
1. Hero
   - Name, title, short intro
   - CTA: Download CV, Contact Me, View Projects
   - Background gradient mesh and animated blobs
2. About / Journey
   - Short biography, mission, timeline of growth
3. Skills
   - Categorized chips and progress bars
4. Featured Projects
   - Three featured cards and one future-project placeholder
5. Certifications / Achievements
   - Timeline cards with emphasis on standout milestones
6. Resume CTA
   - Strong call to action and PDF link
7. Contact
   - Professional form and direct contact links
